Agawa Canyon +
Sault Ste. Marie

Theme: Northern Wilderness ExpeditionAgawaSault Ste. MarieTrain

Agawa gives the age-5 year a second, very different identity: not city energy, but northern wilderness and train adventure. It introduces Canadian Shield scale, long-distance travel, rivers, canyons, fall colour, and the romance of going somewhere remote.
