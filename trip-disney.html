Trip 01 · October 2029 · Age 3 · Preschool · Family Reunion Trip

Disney /

Theme: Wonder Family Reunion Age 3

The first big trip — and the one that plants the seed. Grandma MJ, Grandma LH, and cousin TL join for a full family reunion wrapped around Disney magic, the EPCOT marine pavilion, and Animal Kingdom. Pure joy and first animal memories.

Purpose

Create JJ's first emotional connection with animals and ocean life in a safe, joyful environment. At age 3, learning happens through delight — not lectures. This trip plants seeds that the other seven trips will grow. It is also a rare full-family reunion.

Farm Connection — Why This Works for JJ

JJ already watches animals every day: cattle, horses, goats, chickens, dogs, cats. Disney's Animal Kingdom and EPCOT's Seas pavilion take that same instinct and point it at giraffes, manatees, and sea turtles. The questions won't feel foreign — just aimed at new creatures.

Learning Questions (Age 3)
  • Where does this animal live — farm, jungle, or ocean?
  • What does it eat? Does it hunt or graze?
  • How does it move — swim, run, fly, crawl?
  • Is it big or small compared to our horses?
  • What sound does it make?
Trip Goals
  • First emotional bond with ocean animals
  • Experience animals in spaces beyond the farm
  • Build confidence in new, stimulating places
  • Create shared family memories
  • Plant one "favourite animal" — the hook for everything that follows

Practical Logistics
  • Book Animal Kingdom Lodge 6+ months ahead — savanna rooms sell out fast
  • Disney Genie+ lightning lanes for 2–3 key rides per day
  • Book Turtle Talk with Crush and Coral Reef Restaurant in advance
  • October = shoulder season — lower crowds, tolerable Florida heat
  • Grandparent-friendly pacing: split party option if needed
  • SeaWorld tickets at discount through Costco or AAA
Pre-Trip Prep for JJ
  • Watch Finding Nemo and Finding Dory together before going
  • DK "Eye Wonder: Ocean" — age-appropriate ocean animal book
  • Short David Attenborough Blue Planet clips on YouTube
  • Visit a local zoo or aquarium to warm up observation skills
  • Practice the "favourite animal" question at the farm first
Note for Grandma MJ, Grandma LH & TL

Disney works perfectly as a reunion because every age has something. Grandparents can do slower pavilions while CJ/MJ handle intense rides. Coral Reef Restaurant dinner is the ideal full-family evening — everyone eats surrounded by the aquarium. TL is great company for JJ on rides Grandmas skip.
