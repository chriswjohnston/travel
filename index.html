The roadmap in one paragraph

What makes this plan special is that it is not just a list of vacations. It is a layered progression through wonder, water, wildlife, engineering, climate, geography, and returning to places with deeper understanding. Waterfalls become hydrology, whales become ecology, aquariums become field observation, racing becomes systems thinking, and repeated places become markers of growth. By the high-school graduation trip, JJ will have physically experienced coastlines, oceans, reefs, mountains, fjords, rivers, weather systems, infrastructure, and wilderness as connected parts of the world.
