Niagara Falls +
Welland Canal

Theme: First Natural WonderOntarioHydrologyEngineering

Niagara is the first “how can this be real?” natural wonder. At age 4 it is mostly awe: roaring water, tunnels, boats, mist, and ships in the canal. Later, it becomes a systems lesson in water, power, erosion, infrastructure, and Great Lakes geography.
